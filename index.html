999 Call Simulator

Educational First Aid & Triage Tool

The 999 Call Simulator is a voice-interactive educational tool designed to bridge the gap between First Aid theory and real-world application. It simulates a high-pressure emergency call, forcing the user to verbally communicate with an AI operator. Unlike standard multiple-choice quizzes, this simulator requires the user to speak clearly, answer specific triage questions, and react to changing medical situations in real-time. It is designed to practice the Primary Survey (DR ABC), Secondary Survey (S.A.M.P.L.E / AVPU), and specific Outdoor First Aid protocols.

Core Interaction

  • Universal Emergency Dialing: Accepts major international emergency numbers.
  • Voice Recognition & Text-to-Speech: App listens to spoken answers and responds verbally.
  • Fallback Text Input: Manual text entry for noisy environments.

Note: This is a simulation and not a real emergency service connection.
